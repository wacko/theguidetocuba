Mote & Cuba

Mote & Cuba

To use Mote in our application, we have to require the cuba/mote file and include the Cuba::Mote plugin that comes with it. If we look closely, this has already been done in the app.rb file:

require "cuba"
require "cuba/mote"

...

Cuba.plugin(Cuba::Mote)

The Cuba::Mote plugin is one of the useful extensions provided by the cuba-contrib gem, one of the gems we installed in the section Managing Dependencies.
